## Introduction

This web application is designed to predict an individual's risk of developing diabetes based on several input factors, including age, gender, weight, height, blood pressure, glucose level, and family history of diabetes. It provides a quick assessment and recommendation.

## Features

- Predict diabetes risk based on BMI and glucose level.
- User-friendly interface for inputting data.
- Display of prediction results and recommendations.
- Ability to check or uncheck the Family History of Diabetes checkbox.
