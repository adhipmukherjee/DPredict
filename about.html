About the Web App

The Diabetes Prediction web app is a tool designed to predict an individual's risk of developing diabetes based on various factors such as age, gender, body mass index (BMI), blood pressure, glucose level, and family history of diabetes.

The app utilizes a machine learning model trained on a dataset of diabetes patients. The model has been trained to classify individuals as either having a high risk or a low risk of developing diabetes based on the provided inputs.

How to Use

To use the prediction tool, navigate to the "Prediction Tool" section of the web app. Fill in the required information, such as age, gender, BMI, blood pressure, glucose level, and indicate if there is a family history of diabetes. Upon submitting the form, the model will generate a prediction of your risk of developing diabetes.

Data Privacy

We take data privacy seriously. The information you provide in the prediction tool is solely used for the purpose of generating the prediction result. We do not store or share any personally identifiable information.

About the Dataset and Model

The machine learning model used in this web app has been trained on a carefully curated dataset of diabetes patients. The dataset contains various health-related attributes of individuals and whether they have been diagnosed with diabetes or not. The model has been trained to make predictions based on this historical data, but please note that it may not be 100% accurate for all individuals.
